Total: 15 minutes

Servings: 2

# Ingredients

[[UnitConversions | Unit Conversions]]

- [ ] 1/2 cup uncooked Couscous
- [ ] 2Tbsp Olive oil
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] 3/4 cup Water

# Steps

1. Heat 2 Tbsp olive oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat
1. Add Couscous to pan until lightly browned
1. Add water
1. Add salt to taste
1. Bring water to a boil
1. Cover, and reduce heat to low until all water is absorbed
