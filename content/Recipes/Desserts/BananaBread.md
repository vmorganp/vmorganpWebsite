# Banana Bread

Source:

## Time

| Prep Time | Cook Time   | Total Time  | Servings |
| --------- | ----------- | ----------- | -------- |
| 25 mins   | 1hr 15 mins | 1hr 40 mins | 12 loaf  |

## Ingredients

[[UnitConversions | Unit Conversions]]

- [ ] 3 to 4 very ripe bananas
- [ ] 1 1/4 cups sugar
- [ ] 1/2 cup salted butter, softened
- [ ] 2 large eggs
- [ ] 1/2 cup buttermilk
- [ ] 1 teaspoon vanilla
- [ ] 2 1/2 cups All Purpose Flour
- [ ] 1 teaspoon baking soda
- [ ] 1 teaspoon salt

## Optional

- [ ] 1 cup chopped nuts
- [ ] 1 cup chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350Â°F
1. Grease bottom of 9x5x3 loaf pan
1. Mash 1.5 cups of bananas
1. Beat sugar and butter in bowl
1. Add eggs and beat until smooth
1. Add bananas buttermilk and vanilla. Beat until smooth
1. Stir in flour, baking soda and salt until moistened (lumps are fine)
1. Stir in nuts
1. Pour into pan
1. Bake 1hr 15 minutes
